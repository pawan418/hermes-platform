# Hermes AI Administration Guide

Administration commands for AI integrations and LLMs.

## Commands
- `hermes ai test`: Connectivity testing to OpenAI, Gemini, Ollama, etc.
- `hermes ai chat`: Interactive chat shell in the console.
- `hermes ai models`: Lists models available for active driver.
- `hermes ai providers`: Display active LLM/Embedding configuration drivers.
- `hermes ai embeddings <text>`: Tests vector dimension generation.
- `hermes ai index`: Re-scouts all document assets.
- `hermes ai prompt-test <prompt>`: Measures response latencies.
- `hermes ai benchmark`: Tests tokens-per-second output speeds.
